About Me

I'm an emerging software developer from Seattle, currently working towards a programming internship through the Year Up program. After years of coding scripts for fun since I was a kid, I decided to turn that passion into a career!

I'm well versed in Python programming, and have worked with languages such as HTML, CSS, Javascript, GDScript, Ruby, Lua, C#, and many others. I consider myself to be very adaptable when it comes to translating my experiences from one language to another.

Horrible, frustrating UI is a pet peeve of mine - which is a big part of why developing fast, intuitive and user-friendly UI is exciting for me! It keeps me up at night thinking of all the wasted time and energy that people have put into clicking tedious boxes on their screen, and I've made it my duty to fix that.

If you are seeking someone passionate, motivated and experienced to make your service as efficient and user-friendly as possible, then please reach out to me on GitHub and LinkedIn. You'll be glad you found me!
